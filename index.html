Southern French Toast

Directions

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1-1/2 cups milk
  • dash of nutmeg
  • dash of cinnamon
  • vanilla to taste
  • 6 thick slices Texas toast

Direction

  1. Beat eggs to a creamy texture
  2. Add in milk and mix together
  3. Add in nutmeg, cinnamon and vanilla to taste
  4. Dip toast into mixture
  5. Place toast into a non-stick skillet browning on one side
    6. This will take 2-3 minutes
  6. Repeat process on other side
  7. Serve with powdered sugar or syrup or both on top
